TrustLayer - MVP

📌 Project Summary

TrustLayer is a Telegram Mini App powered by the TON blockchain that evaluates and rewards user behavior and emotional intelligence through daily interactions. Inspired by David R. Hawkins’ map of consciousness, users complete tasks that reflect emotions and receive a Social Trust Score (STS).

🎯 Purpose & Function

- Build a social trust layer in Telegram via gamified tasks
- Score is based on emotional quality of interactions
- Tasks include AI chats, content sharing, and self-reflection prompts
- Scores are saved securely on-chain via smart contracts

💰 Income & Token Utility

- Higher scores unlock paid micro-jobs & VIP access
- Users earn TON or Telegram Stars by completing verified tasks
- Scores serve as social reputation in partner apps
